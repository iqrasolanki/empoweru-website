Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this program for? +

For professionals, managers, entrepreneurs, students or anyone who wants to build confidence.

Is the program online or offline? +

Both! We offer live online sessions, in-person sessions and 1:1 coaching sessions.

How long is the program? +

The program runs for 10–15 weeks, depending on the batch and format. 1:1 coaching is fully customizable.

Will this help if I am introverted or shy? +

Absolutely. Many of our best results come from introverted learners. The training is gentle, supportive and designed to build confidence from within.

What will I learn? +

You’ll learn to communicate assertively, overcome hesitation, shift limiting beliefs using NLP, handle conversations calmly and build a confident personality.

Is there support after the program? +

Yes! You get worksheets, practice exercises, check-ins and optional ongoing coaching for deeper growth.
